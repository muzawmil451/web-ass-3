Favorite Cities

Helsinki

Helsinki, the capital of Finland, serves as a vibrant cultural hub with its beautiful coastline, diverse architecture, and deep appreciation for design. Renowned for its high quality of life and safety, the city offers visitors and residents an impressive blend of historical and modern attractions, including the unique Suomenlinna sea fortress.

Islamabad

Islamabad, the capital city of Pakistan, stands out for its greenery, cleanliness, and well-organized layout. Nestled against the backdrop of the Margalla Hills, it offers numerous recreational activities within its numerous parks and hiking trails. As a planned city, Islamabad is known for its high standards of living, modern infrastructure, and significant landmarks like Faisal Mosque, the largest mosque in Pakistan.

Seoul

Seoul, South Korea's bustling capital, perfectly blends ancient traditions with cutting-edge technology. The city is known for its skyscraper-filled skyline, including the iconic N Seoul Tower, and historic sites like Gyeongbokgung Palace. Seoul's vibrant cultural scene, coupled with its global influence in music and entertainment, makes it a dynamic city to explore.
